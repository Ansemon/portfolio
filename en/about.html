About me

Data Science student at Universidad del Norte (Barranquilla, Colombia), currently on a professional internship at Promigas S.A. E.S.P. in the Mercado y Growth area.

LinkedIn ↗

Why Data Science

Ever since I was a kid, it was always clear I wanted to be a scientist. The hard part was deciding which science, because all of them fascinated me in their own way. When I discovered Data Science, I fell in love with the idea of a science for all sciences: being able to contribute to almost any area of human knowledge seemed wonderful to me.

How I am

I'm a very curious person, with a particular passion for efficiency. I enjoy optimizing processes and finding specific solutions that make things run more efficiently — sometimes to an extreme. Looking ahead, I'm especially interested in applications in business and finance, as well as biology, with a particular interest in time series analysis.
